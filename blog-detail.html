This is what hope tastes like.

BLOGS by #thegirlwhodecides 05Jan, 2023

Sipping water from one of those tall glasses, she thought to herself about the design of the poster. The poster that needs to go on social media calling out expressions of interest from humans in Melbourne. She believed this will integrate into a podcast series in February. Then again, she thought about the design of the website for her renovation business, something that is still fresh as a daisy.

While her side hustle with the media was on a pause, she ran into a mutual friend from the media at lunch this afternoon. That is shockingly unusual for Melbourne, running into friends in such a large city. So then she thought to herself, is this the universe sent sign for her to uncaused the media gig as well?
Accompanied by this chaotic brain, she rocked up to her day job as an engineering advisor wearing her flowery pink dress.
